RSD charter schools eligible for transfer to OPSB

Recovery School District 9-12 grade charter high schools that earned a school performance score of 54 or higher in 2014 and 2015 are eligible to return to the Orleans Parish School Board.

School performance score letter grades align with the following scale:
A: 100.0 – 150.0
B: 85.0 – 99.9
C: 70.0 – 84.9
D: 47.1 – 69.9
F: 0.0 – 47.0
